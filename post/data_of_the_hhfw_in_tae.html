Data of the HHFW in TAE

Chao yu, qingxi ynag, yuntao song, jiahao li, hao xu, xiaokang yang, michl binderbauer, jon schroeder, yuanxu song, richard goulding, bart van compernolle, troy carter, ning li, yongsheng wang, wei song Oct 20, 2022, Hhfw antenna components deformation results simulation calculation electric field distribution optimized structure s11 parameters k-spectrum results

The data includes the overall structure of the HHFW antenna, as well as all components. It also includes the deformation results of the antenna in the simulation calculation, the electric field distribution of the optimized structure, the S11 parameters and the K-spectrum results.

数据包括HHFW天线的整体结构以及所有组件。还包括天线在仿真计算中的变形结果、优化结构的电场分布、S11参数和K谱结果。
