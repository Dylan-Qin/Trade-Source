Chemostratigraphic data and compilation of nitrogen isotope data from the Ediacaran to early Cambrian

Hansheng cao, zaiyun wang, fajin chen, kai kai, hanli huang, qiuping chen, chunqing chen Apr 28, 2025, Nitrogen isotope data South china Published papers Geologic age estimates Ediacaran Early cambrian period

Nitrogen isotope data reported from South China in published papers are compiled,and geologic age estimates of nitrogen isotopes from Ediacaran to the early Cambrian period.

已发表的论文中报道的中国南方氮同位素数据被汇编，并估算了从埃迪卡拉纪到早寒武世的氮同位素地质年龄。
